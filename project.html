Whatever the values or weather the fruit is fresh or not sent on our website. Then we can easily analysis our report.

Present robots are classified into different categories based on their features and performance like industrial robots, commercial robots, service robots, mobile robots. Smart India Hackathon 2019 (SIH-2019)

Low cost Automatic Dishwasher Machine

As we know that 21st century is moving very fastly towards Automation so Automation is the todays main area of working. We are going to develop a machine based on engineering logics and tricks, to reduce the efforts and to save precious time of human being. To move towards the least crisis of budget we will provide Low Cost Automatic Dishwasher Machine which will have effective cleanliness of dishes ,low usage of water and power saviour features .Wastage of water can be minimized by recycling water by using low cost filter. Annoying sound of dishes during washing will be reduced in this machine. Arduino Uno is a micro-controller board used to control the dishwasher machine. When dishes are kept in dishwasher it will sensed by IR sensors and then dish will pick up by gripper from stack of dishes and place in a conveyor belt. In a conveyor belt dish will be placed vertically and hold by side holders . In first stage of dishwasher machine water will sprinkle on dish to remove the waste food particles . In second stage dish moves forward with the help of conveyor belt and wash by scrubber which will contain liquid soap . Scrubber will clean the dishes from both the side. In third stage dish will be clean by water two-three times , also in this stage filtering is done and filtered water is reused to clean food particles in first stage . At last dishes will be dried. When work is done the machine will switched off automatically and it will informed to the user via the Alarm/Buzzer. Operation of machine is noiseless.
